Resources

This area is reserved for paying clients only. To gain access to the archive of quizzes and study sheets, you must have paid for an in-person session in the previous calendar month. New login credentials will be e-mailed to you to maintain access.

These resources will grow as I teach new topics. If I don't have resources available that are pertinent to the topic of an in-person appointment, I will work to generate new content and push it out to you within a week of our meeting.

For clients, think of this space as your quiet library space online. It's not a replacement for your class's resources, but it should serve as way to keep the concepts you've learned and reviewed with me fresh in your mind.

Free Resources

For more review and practice, I recommend Khan Academy. They have a large collection of instructional and practice material for a variety of fields. I also recommend ACT Academy, which goes far beyond just prep for the ACT, bringing the best resources from all over the web for subjects they test, all for free.
