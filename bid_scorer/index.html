Screw the Dealer

The dealer's bid cannot cause the sum of the bids to be equal to the number of cards in the hand.

Incorrect/Incomplete Bids

Make sure all players have recorded bids and that the total sum of the bids does not equal HAND SIZE.

Incorrect/Incomplete Outcomes

Make sure all players have recorded outcomes of the hand.

Invalid Setup

Make sure you have names for all of your players, and that there are enough cards in a 52-card deck for that number of players with the maximum number of cards dealt.
