How to Use Pepper Scorer

Welcome to Pepper Scorer in the Cloud!

This tool is meant to assist you in keeping score of your game of Pepper. Everything should be self-explanatory, but brief explainers follow.

Entering Player Names

You enter in the names of the the four people playing, starting with the first dealer and then continuing in clockwise order. Press return or click the "Awesome, my player names are right!" button when you are done with player names.

Entering Team Names

Similarly, you will then be asked to enter in team names. Do this and press enter or click on the "Super, my team names are right!" button to proceed to the game.

Gameplay

Then the game begins with the pepper round. The current scores should always be in the top (smaller screens) or top left (larger screens), and instructions for what you should do next are right below that, along with a series of buttons indicating the next actions you might take. Simply press the appropriate button after players have made their decision to move the game along.

As the game progresses, a more detailed hand-by-hand score sheet is maintained below the instructions (smaller screens) or to the right (larger screens) that indicates the hand number of the most recent hand, who dealt it, the scores after that hand, and the bidder, bid value, and suit of the bid.

After one team has won, a button should appear allowing you to restart the game. If you press it, the scores will be rezeroed, the score sheet will be emptied, and a new pepper round starts with the same person as in the first game.

Things to Note

This is all happening in one web page. So, if you press the back button, you will lose everything. In short, never push the back button. Instead, if you make a mistake, use the left-most back-looking button in the gameplay area to go back one or more steps.
